About

Robert Lemos

Veteran technology journalist of more than 25 years. Former research engineer exploring AI applications in early 1990s. Written for more than two dozen publications, including CNET News.com, Dark Reading, MIT’s Technology Review, Popular Science, and Wired News. Five awards for journalism, including the Society of Professional Journalist’s Sigma Delta Chi award for Best Deadline Journalism (Online) in 2003 for coverage of the Blaster worm. Crunches numbers on various trends using Python and R. Recent reports include analyses of the shortage in cybersecurity workers and annual vulnerability trends. Writes technically deep, yet engaging, whitepapers and reports for a select group of corporate clients.
