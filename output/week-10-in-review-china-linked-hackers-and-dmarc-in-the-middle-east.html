"Week 10 in Review: China-linked hackers and DMARC in the Middle East"

March 08, 2024 by Robert Lemos

- week-in-review

A targeted watering-hole cyberattack linked to a Chinese threat group infected visitors to a Buddhism festival website, while organizations worldwide have quickly adopted a trio of email authentication technologies, with some firms in the Middle East adopting them as quickly.

China-Linked Cyber Spies Blend Watering Hole, Supply Chain Attacks

Middle East Leads in Deployment of DMARC Email Security
